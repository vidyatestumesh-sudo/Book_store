About the Retreat

Mastering Life is more than a retreat — it is a 360° wellness experience that nourishes the whole being — mind, body, and soul. Each day blends stillness and movement, silence and sharing, wisdom and practice — creating a rhythm that carries you beyond concepts and into lived transformation.

Through guided meditation, you quiet the mind and find your center. Through conscious movement, qi gong, and breathwork, you reconnect with the body, releasing tension and restoring flow. Through customized nutrition, you rebalance your system and transform your health. And through energy healing, sound baths, and ecstatic dance, you open pathways for joy and authentic living.

With the power of Pyramid Valley’s sacred setting, you will reset your nervous system, release old patterns, and bring you into presence, clarity, and flow. You’ll return not only rested, but realigned — with yourself, with life, and with the deeper intelligence that moves us all.

Nutrition by Ati ™

Pyramid Valley International

Imagine stepping away from the noise of daily life into a serene valley surrounded by gentle hills and lush greenery. Pyramid Valley International, just outside Bangalore, is home to the world’s largest meditation pyramid — a 100-foot sanctuary of sacred energy designed to accelerate inner stillness and clarity. Many visitors describe the experience as stepping into another dimension of peace.

Spread across 40 acres, the campus is dotted with powerful energy spots, quiet spaces for reflection, and state-of-the-art halls for workshops and gatherings. Here, you’ll find the perfect balance of silence, learning, and transformation.

Accommodations range from simple dormitories to eco-luxury suites, all created to support your inner journey in a distraction-free environment. Alongside this, guests are nourished with wholesome sattvic vegan and vegetarian meals, crafted to keep the body light and the mind clear.

When you join our retreat here, you’re not just attending an event — you’re immersing yourself in a sacred environment designed to help you deepen your practice, release old patterns, and awaken a profound sense of inner freedom.

FAQ

Will I need a visa to enter India?

Yes. Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter India, including for tourism and short visits. The easiest option is the e-Tourist Visa (e-TV), which can be applied for online. We recommend applying well in advance on the official Indian government eVisa portal.

Do I need vaccinations to enter India?

For most visitors, no special vaccinations are required. The exception is if you are traveling from a country where yellow fever is present (mainly in parts of Africa or South America) — in that case, proof of vaccination is required on arrival.

Food and water in India may differ from what you’re used to. For extended travel outside of Bangalore or rural stays, consult your doctor about additional vaccinations or precautions.

How do I get to Pyramid Valley International?

  • From Kempegowda International Airport (Bangalore): ~90 km / 2.5–3 hrs by taxi

  • From Bangalore City Center: ~35 km / 1–1.5 hrs by taxi

Taxis and ride-sharing apps are widely available, including Uber. Airport transfers are included and will be organized separately closer to the retreat dates.

Are airport transfers included?

Yes. Round-trip transfers to and from Kempegowda International Airport (BLR) are included. Prior to the retreat, we’ll collect your flight details to coordinate smooth pickups and drop-offs so you can relax from the moment you arrive.

What language will the retreat be conducted in?

All sessions are conducted in English. Support is available in Hindi, German, Russian, and Persian. Attendees from around the world bring many other languages into the mix.

Can I bring my children?

Yes. Children ages 6–18 can join the dedicated Youth Learning Center, with trained facilitators leading activities such as meditation, nature hikes, Adventure Park sessions, and even special visits from Cirak.

To maintain Noble Silence and support adult participants, children remain in their own program and do not mingle with the retreat group (except in urgent situations). Parents can fully immerse, while children enjoy a parallel program of growth and play.

What should I pack?

Pack light and bring essentials:

  • Personal toiletries and medications

  • Light, breathable clothing (cotton/linen) for meditation & movement

  • A sweater or shawl for cooler mornings/evenings

  • Comfortable casual shoes + sturdy walking shoes for nature trails

What is the electronics policy?

To preserve presence:

  • No phone or device use during Noble Silence (6:00 am–12:30 pm daily).

  • After 12:30 pm, phones and computers may be used sparingly for photos, short messages, or urgent needs.

  • We encourage mindful sharing — connect without losing presence.

What if I have food allergies or dietary needs?

Pyramid Valley has a strictly vegan and vegetarian kitchen, where dairy is included, but no eggs or other animal proteins are used.

For that reason, we’ve gone the extra mile to ensure all meals are thoughtfully designed to suit all body types and dietary needs — within the vegan and vegetarian framework — so you feel nourished, balanced, and well cared for.

Once you are here, our kitchen team is happy to accommodate allergies and restrictions — just let us know, and we’ll ensure your needs are met with care.

What’s included (and what’s not)?

Your stay at Pyramid Valley is fully inclusive:

  • Accommodation in your selected room type

  • Three nourishing meals daily (vegan and vegetarian)

  • All teachings, workshops, and activities

  • Access to Pyramid Valley’s sacred spaces and natural surroundings

  • Round-trip airport transfers

Not included: international airfare, travel insurance, and personal expenses. Everything else is thoughtfully arranged so you can simply arrive, settle in, and immerse yourself in the journey without concern for hidden costs.

Can I arrive early or stay longer?

Yes. Extended stays can be arranged (based on availability). Pyramid Valley offers a pool, fine dining, and the Soul Adventure Park with activities like paintball, rock climbing, archery, human soccer, and more. Contact us via email or WhatsApp to arrange an early or extended stay.

Can I travel around India afterwards?

Absolutely. Popular post-retreat options include:

  • Auroville (Pondicherry, East India): experimental spiritual community

  • Rishikesh (North India): yoga capital near the Himalayas and the Ganga

  • Goa (West India): laid-back beaches and vibrant culture

  • Maldives: a one-hour flight south for pristine islands and ocean

All can be reached via Bangalore (BLR) airport.

What is the refund policy?

  • Cancellations made up to 60 days before the retreat (Nov 28, 2025) are eligible for a 50% refund. No refunds are issued thereafter.
  • Payment plans are offered for your convenience, but the same cancellation policy applies. If you cancel more than 60 days before start, you are responsible for 50% of the full retreat fee, whether or not that amount has yet been paid through your installment plan.
  • If you arrange for another attendee to take your spot, you’ll receive a 100% refund at any time, once their booking is confirmed.
  • For double occupancy bookings, if one guest cancels, the remaining guest will be rebooked at the single occupancy rateand is responsible for the balance.
  • If you choose to leave the event early, no refunds or credits will be issued.
